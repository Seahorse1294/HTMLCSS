Upcoming Events

Event 1
 The Warwick Chess Community Club is excited to host the "Perth Chess Masters Tournament" on May 15th, 2023. Top-rated chess players from across Western Australia will compete for the grand prize of $5000. Spectators are welcome to watch the action live at our venue in Warwick, Perth.
Event 2
 On June 20th, 2023, the Warwick Chess Community Club will host a simultaneous exhibition by Grandmaster Magnus Carlsen. This is a unique opportunity to play against the current world chess champion and learn from his exceptional skills. Limited spots are available, so register early to secure your place.
Event 3
 The Warwick Chess Community Club is proud to present the "Junior Chess Championship" on July 25th, 2023. This event is open to all junior players under the age of 18, and there will be multiple age categories for participants. The winners will receive trophies and prizes, and all participants will receive a certificate of participation.
