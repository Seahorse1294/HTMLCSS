About Us

Warwick Community Chess Club is a non-profit organization that aims to promote the game of chess in Warwick and the surrounding areas. Our club is open to chess enthusiasts of all skill levels and ages, from beginners to seasoned players. We believe that chess is a fun and engaging game that offers numerous benefits, including improved critical thinking, problem-solving, and concentration skills.

Our club was founded by a group of passionate chess players who wanted to create a welcoming and supportive community for those who share their love for the game. We offer a friendly and inclusive environment where members can learn, practice, and play chess together. Our members come from diverse backgrounds, and we value diversity, inclusivity, and respect for all.

At Warwick Community Chess Club, we organize regular meetings, tournaments, and events to foster a sense of community and promote the game of chess. Our experienced coaches offer lessons and training sessions for those who want to improve their skills, while our casual events provide a relaxed and fun atmosphere for players of all levels to enjoy.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced player, we invite you to join our community and discover the joys of chess with us. We are dedicated to providing a supportive and welcoming environment for all chess enthusiasts, and we look forward to meeting you at one of our events soon!

Club History

Warwick Community Chess Club was founded in 1998 with the aim of promoting chess in the Warwick community and providing a place for chess enthusiasts to come together and play. Over the years, the club has grown in membership and has become a hub for chess players of all levels, from beginners to experienced players. The club has organized several tournaments and events, including the annual Warwick Open, which attracts players from across the state. The club is proud of its long-standing tradition of sportsmanship, fair play, and camaraderie.

Club Mission

The mission of Warwick Community Chess Club is to promote the game of chess in the Warwick community and provide a welcoming and supportive environment for chess enthusiasts of all levels. We strive to offer opportunities for our members to improve their skills, participate in tournaments, and meet other players who share their passion for the game. We believe that chess is not only a challenging and rewarding game but also a great way to build social connections and foster a sense of community.

Club Future

As we look to the future, Warwick Community Chess Club is committed to continuing its tradition of excellence in promoting chess in the community. We plan to expand our outreach efforts to encourage more people to learn and play chess. We also aim to establish partnerships with local schools and organizations to provide chess education and training to young players. We will continue to organize tournaments and events, and we hope to attract more players from the region and beyond. With the support of our members and the community, we are confident that Warwick Community Chess Club will continue to thrive and grow for many years to come.
